About Me

Hello! My name is Devin, and I am a researcher in linguistics based at Northwestern University. I completed my undergraduate studies in linguistics and computer science at University of Wisconsin - Madison, and I am soon (June 2021) to receive my master’s degree in computational linguistics from University of Washington. At Washington, I have been affiliated with the CLMBR Lab led by Shane Steinert-Threlkeld, focusing on questions at the intersection of cognitive science and semantics. In addition, I have worked with the Center for an Informed Public in a directed research group led by Kolina Koltai, focusing on vaccine/mask wearing narratives on social media during the COVID-19 pandemic. After my graduation from Washington in June, I will be transitioning to research in collaboration with Rob Voigt at Northwestern University.
