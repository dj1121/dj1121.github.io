About

I'm a PhD student in linguistics at Northwestern University. My main research interests are in computational psycholinguistics, (morpho-)syntax, and East-Asian languages. I also teach and/or TA undergraduate and graduate classes in linguistics and ESL. My background is in both linguistics and computer science. I completed my BS at UW-Madison and my MS at University of Washington.

Outside of work my two favorite things are probably video games and playing music. I'm a big fan of strategy and cooperative games (tactical shooters, RTS, etc.). As for music, my favorite genre has been jazz for a long time, but really I listen to just about all genres.
