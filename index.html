About

I'm a PhD student in linguistics at Northwestern University with background in both linguistics and computer science. My work combines syntactic theory with psycholinguistics to study the mechanisms of human sentence processing. In other words, what knowledge do people have about words and how they can combine into sentences (i.e., syntax)? Additionally, how does the mind put this knowledge to use in real time in order to comprehend sentences (i.e., processing)?

Most of my studies now focus on experiments and modelling to see if/how people use the knowledge they have about their language to predict what comes next in a sentence (i.e., prediction). The work extends across various languages, including English, Japanese, Russian, etc.

Besides my research, I do various other things like tutoring, translation and programming. In my free time, I like video games, being in nature, learning mathematics/other languages, and discovering new music. For more details about my services, please take a look here.

Translation

I offer technical and academic translation, proofreading, editing services. I currently focus on projects from Japanese and/or Italian into English. Topics I cover include computer software, computer science, linguistics, academic research, video games, psychology, and more. Please contact me to see if your project is a good fit.

Tutoring

I have experience teaching various subjects at both high school and college levels. I have done both individual tutoring and have led lectures as well. Topics I can teach include, but are not limited to: math, English, foreign languages, and computer science. If you're not sure if I can help, just ask!

In addition I am a certified TESOL/TEFL instructor. Most of my teaching in this area relates to training foreign graduate students to be able to teach undergraduate courses. In this regard, I have most experience with Chinese, Japanese, and Korean native speakers.

Technical Consulting

If you have questions for a project about programming, language technologies, etc., I am happy to help. I have experience in several programming languages and tools such as Python, R, Java, etc., and I have implemented various systems to collect and analyze data.
